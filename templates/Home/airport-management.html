Diploma in Air Hostess:

Eagleaviation Institute offers an Air Hostess Diploma. Classroom lectures and practical training sessions make up the academic programme. The programme prepares learners to become competent airline employees. As per the general public, airlines employ a flight attendant to offer food and beverages to their travellers! This assertion, on the other hand, is incorrect! The job of an air hostess entails more than just offering drinks and meals.

We also give training to airhostess to make them expert in conducting many duties such as

  • Confirming the security and comfort of passengers
  • Conveying flight announce and good coordination within department
  • Serving food, drinks, books, and other basic things to travellers
  • Helping passengers to follow safety guidelines in the flight
  • Helping passengers at the time of panic situation, emergency etc
